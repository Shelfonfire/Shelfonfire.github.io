# Open Cinema

## Overview

Discover film screenings around you in London. Get personalised suggestions based on your preferences, browse real-time showtime data from 50+ independent cinemas, and find what's playing nearby on an interactive map.

## Key Features

- Personalised screening suggestions based on your taste and location
- Real-time film and screening data from 50+ London cinemas
- Interactive Leaflet map with marker clustering
- Filter by date, time range, genre tags, and free-text search
- Aggregated data from multiple cinema booking platforms

## Tech Stack

Next.js 14 frontend with Leaflet/CARTO tiles and TailwindCSS. FastAPI backend serving from SQLite via Mangum for Lambda deployment.
